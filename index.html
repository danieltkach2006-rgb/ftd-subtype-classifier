Genetic Testing Results
TARDBP encodes the TDP-43 protein. Mutations are associated with TDP-43 proteinopathy and frequently co-occur with ALS.
MAPT encodes the Tau protein. Mutations are strongly associated with Tau-type FTD (Pick's disease).
GRN mutations cause progranulin deficiency, associated with TDP-43 proteinopathy and often language-variant FTD.
Most common genetic cause of familial FTD and ALS. Associated with TDP-43 pathology.
